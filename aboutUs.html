Defining the modern classic

Reyani is a world-renowned, award-winning designer of luxury accessories and ready-to-wear. This namesake company, established in 1981, currently produces a range of products under the signature Reyani Collection, Reyani and Reyani luxe labels. These products include accessories, footwear, watches, jewelry, women’s and men’s ready-to-wear, wearable technology, eyewear and a full line of fragrance products.

Our History

Behind this burgeoning empire stands a singular designer with an innate sense of glamour and an unfailing eye for timeless chic. Reyani has won numerous accolades within the fashion industry, been honored for his philanthropy, and earned the respect and affection of millions. Wholly dedicated to a vision of style that is as sophisticated as it is indulgent, as iconic as it is modern, he has created an enduring luxury lifestyle empire with a global reach.
